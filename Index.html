The Beatles ere an english rock bandformed in liverpool in 1960. With a line-up comprising john Lennon, paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr, They are regarded as the most influential band of all time

The group were integral to the evolution of pop music into an art form and to the development of the counterculture of the 1960s.
